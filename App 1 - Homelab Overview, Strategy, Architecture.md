# App 1 - Homelab Overview, Strategy, Architecture


## The Homelab and the Handbook

The intent is to create a unified system to setup and manage a home lab development environment containing a plethora of machines, both real and virtual, across a collection of operating systems that will include Windows, Linux, Android and others

This specification serves as the comprehensive guide and requirements document for developing an adaptive, flexible automation solution for provisioning and managing a diverse development infrastructure. The implementation and ongoing management and maintenance of this Homelab will be realized through a series of Ansible scripts, meticulously defined and documented by this collection of documents that will be called the Handbook.

The ultimate deliverables will include:

1. Ansible playbooks
2. All required configuration files
3. A comprehensive handbook describing system setup, usage, management and maintenance
   This handbook will start off as a series of markdown files
